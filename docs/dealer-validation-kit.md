# UsedCarsNZ Dealer Discovery Kit

> Purpose: Validate the core assumptions behind UsedCarsNZ before building:
>
> - Will dealers trial a second platform?
> - Is response-time pain real?
> - How frustrated are dealers with Trade Me?
> - Would dealers eventually pay?
> - Is enquiry volume high enough for the conversion model to work?

---

# 1. Dealer Interview Script

## Introduction

Hi, I'm researching a new platform for NZ car dealers.

Nothing is built yet and I'm not selling anything today.

I'm trying to understand how dealers currently manage leads, what frustrates them about Trade Me, and whether faster lead response would actually help.

Would you mind if I asked a few questions?

# 2. Follow-Up Questions

## Response Time Pain

- How many enquiries arrive outside business hours?
- What percentage are answered the next day?
- Do you currently measure response times?
- How many leads do you think go cold before contact?
- What happens when your sales staff are busy?

---

## Trade Me Frustration

- Have Trade Me costs increased for your dealership?
- How much do you spend per month?
- Have you considered leaving?
- What alternatives have you tried?
- Why didn't those alternatives work?

---

## Lead Quality

- What percentage of enquiries are genuine?
- What percentage become appointments?
- What percentage become sales?
- What information would help qualify buyers better?

---

## Enquiry Volume

- How many enquiries does a typical vehicle receive?
- Which vehicles generate the most enquiries?
- Which generate almost none?
- Do you have enough enquiry volume to justify additional software?

---

## Trial Adoption

- What would you need to see before trying another platform?
- Would free onboarding matter?
- Would DMS integration be required?
- Would manual uploads be acceptable initially?
- How much effort would you invest in testing a new platform?

---

## Pricing

- What dealer software do you currently pay for?
- How do you judge ROI?
- What price would feel expensive?
- What price would feel like a no-brainer?

# 3. Objection Handling Guide

## Objection: "We already pay Trade Me."

Response:

> That's completely fair.
>
> The idea isn't to replace Trade Me.
>
> I'm trying to understand whether a second platform that improves conversion from existing enquiries would be valuable.

---

## Objection: "We don't need AI."

Response:

> That's useful feedback.
>
> The real question I'm trying to understand is whether faster responses and better-qualified leads would help.
>
> AI is simply one possible way of delivering that.

---

## Objection: "We already answer enquiries quickly."

Response:

> That's great.
>
> How do you currently achieve that?
>
> Do you have any numbers around your response times?

---

## Objection: "We tried another platform before."

Response:

> What happened?
>
> Was it:
>
> - Lack of traffic?
> - Poor lead quality?
> - Too much admin?
> - Difficult setup?
> - Something else?

---

## Objection: "We won't pay for another listing site."

Response:

> Understood.
>
> If a platform could prove additional appointments or sales, what would need to happen before it became worth paying for?

# Phase 0 Success Criteria

Proceed to MVP build if:

- 4/5 dealers report meaningful Trade Me frustration.
- 3/5 dealers acknowledge slow response loses sales.
- Average enquiry volume supports the conversion model.
- 3/5 dealers agree to trial.
- 2+ dealers indicate willingness to pay after proven results.
- 2+ dealers volunteer for a pilot.
